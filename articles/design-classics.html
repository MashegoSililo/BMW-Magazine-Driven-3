Design isn't everything - but everything else is nothing without good design. What's more, design is constantly changing. Just like BMW, Mies van der Rohe's Barcelona Chair and Le Corbusier's LC4 chaise longue now seem certain to survive the test of time. But what of more recent design classics? We asked a jury of experts to appraise outstanding product designs since the year 2000. A few of our sugggestions failed to make the shortlist: the Lego show by Balenciaga, the paper bag by Saskia Diez and Moooi's Brave New World wooden lamp. But as you are about discover on the pages that follow: design is "life styling".
